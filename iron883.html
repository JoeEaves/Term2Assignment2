Iron 883.

The Iron 883 comes with loads of new features like, low seat, slammed suspension, tuck and roll seat, 9-spoke wheels and loads more cool features.


The Iron 883 has a blacked out look with a black powder coated 883cc engine with black rocker covers.


The Iron 883 has street inspired paint meaning it has colours which just look right agains the urban back drop. The bike also has a tough finish meaning it is hard to scratch and you dont need to worry about catching it on anything.


The mix of a lowered front and rear suspension with a narrow solo seat only 760mm high provides confidence and confort for riders of all sizes and experience.


The tuck and roll seat is combined modern comfort with a tuck and roll design which takes you back to the early days of bobbers.


The 883cc Harley engine dripping with power rides smooth but keeps the sound and look of a classic Harley Davidson. The engione will run smooth for thousands and thousands of miles.


The drag style bars are an old school look in black to top off the amazing looking bike and giving the rider an aggressive riding position.
