SofTail Deluxe.

The Deluxe 2018 comes with loads of new features such as the retro-modern LED lights, 107 v-twin engine and many more cool new features.


The 107 v-Twin Engine delivers power smoothness and a crisp throttle along with the satisfying rumble of a Harley Davidson.


The pull-back handlebars offer easy reach for the rider and has a chrome pullback riser that gets the grips even closer and gives the front end a aerodynamic look.


The chrome laced wheels with whitewall tires gives the bike the retro look it needs and deserves sending the bike back to the '50s.


The retro-modern style of LED lights allow the rider to go on night rides and be able to see and be seen with LED headlights, running lights, turn signals and brake lights.


The new Softail frame offers a lighter, stiffer frame compared to the older Softail and Dyna Deluxes.


The new high perfoprmance front suspension provides the performance of a racing style cartridge fork but with less weight.


The all new Mono rear shock suspension allows for dymanic cornering capability while keeping the classic look of a hardtail.
