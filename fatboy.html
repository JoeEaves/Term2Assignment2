Fat Boy.

The Fat Boy 2018 is the best Fat Boy they have come out with yet with loads of new fetures like: Satin chrome finishes, Steamroller stance, Milwaukee-eight big twin engine, Signature LED forward lighting, All-new softail frame and high performance front suspension and rear mono shock.


The Satin chrome finish gives the Fat Boy a look that will impress any crowd.


The Steamroller stance gives the Fat Boy a muscular look thanks to the new solid disc Lakester wheels wrapped in the widest front tire they have done yet from the factory at 160mm and a 240mm rear tire.


The Big Twin Engine delivers power smoothness and a crisp throttle along with the classic rumble from any Fat Boy.


The LED forward light gives the rider an amazing riding experience on the darkest of nights.


The new Softail frame offers a lighter, stiffer frame compared to the older Softail and Dyna Fat Boys.


The new high perfoprmance front suspension provides the performance of a racing style cartridge fork but with less weight.


The all new Mono rear shock suspension allows for dymanic cornering capability while keeping the classic look of a hardtail.
