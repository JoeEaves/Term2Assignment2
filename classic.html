Heritage Classic 2018.

The Heritage Classic 2018 is the best Classic they have come out with yet with loads of new fetures like: Detachable windscreen, cruise control, Milwaukee-eight big twin engine, Signature LED forward lighting, All-new softail frame and high performance front suspension and rear mono shock.


The Big Twin Engine delivers power smoothness and a crisp throttle along with the classic rumble from any Harley Davidson.


The LED forward light gives the rider an amazing riding experience on the darkest of nights.


The new Softail frame offers a lighter, stiffer frame compared to the older Softail and Dyna Heritage Classics.


The Heritage classic comes with a lockable and sealed saddlebag each side of the bike.


The new high perfoprmance front suspension provides the performance of a racing style cartridge fork but with less weight.


The all new Mono rear shock suspension allows for dymanic cornering capability while keeping the classic look of a hardtail.


Go from touring to cruising in seconds with the new detachable windscreen. Leave the screen on for a clean look or remove it in seconds for a completely new look for your bike.


The Heritage Classic comes with a standard cruise control. All you have to do is enter in your speed and give your wrist a rest on those long rides we all love to go on.
