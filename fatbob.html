Fat Bob.

The 2018 Fat Bob comes with loads of cool new features a small selection of these are, Inverted forks, dual disk front brakes, 2-1-2 exaust and many more features that will want any motorbike guy want one.


The new inverted front forks deliver the most precise handling a rider could ask for.


The engine is not short on power and these high performance dual disk brakes help the rider keep that power in check when needed.


The 2-1-2 exhaust is a big performance inspired exhaust upswept with an amazing custom contrast finish.


The bold aluminum wheels have the most aggressive fat tires give the rider asphalt eating traction for those quick starts.


The Big Twin Engine delivers power smoothness and a crisp throttle along with the classic rumble from any Fat Bob.


The LED lights give the rider an amazing riding experience on the darkest of nightsand during the day making sure all eyes are focused on you the rider.


The new high perfoprmance front suspension provides the performance of a racing style cartridge fork but with less weight.


The all new Mono rear shock suspension allows for dymanic cornering capability while keeping the classic look of a hardtail.
